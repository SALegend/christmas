Jesus

Jesus, also known as Jesus of Nazareth, was a Jewish religious leader who is revered as the Son of God and the savior of humanity in the Abrahamic religions. According to the New Testament, Jesus was born in Bethlehem, Palestine, to Mary, a virgin, and was fathered by God. He was a central figure in the life and teachings of the Christian church, and his life and teachings are the basis for the Christian religion. Jesus is also an important figure in Islam, and is considered to be one of the greatest prophets in that religion.
